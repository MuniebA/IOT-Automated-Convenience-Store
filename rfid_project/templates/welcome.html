How to Use the Smart Cart

1

Start a Shopping Session

Begin by entering your name and address to start a new shopping session. If you're a returning customer, we'll find your previous information automatically.

2

Scan Products

Simply place your product's RFID tag near the scanner. The system will automatically detect the product and add it to your cart.

RFID scanning demonstration

3

Review Your Items

Our system will automatically validate scanned items and display them in your cart. Confirm each item before proceeding to checkout.

4

Checkout

When you're done shopping, proceed to checkout. The system will generate a receipt with all your purchased items.
